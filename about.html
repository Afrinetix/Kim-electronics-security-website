Our Journey

8 Years of Protecting Kenya

Kim Electronics & Security Solutions was founded in 2016 by a team of passionate electrical and security engineers who saw a clear gap in the Kenyan market: too many substandard installations done by unqualified individuals, leaving homes and businesses vulnerable.

We set out with a simple but powerful mission: provide the highest quality security and electrical services at fair prices, every single time. We invested in the best equipment, trained our team to manufacturer certification standards, and built a culture of accountability and excellence.

Today, eight years later, we've completed over 3,200 projects across Kenya, from single-camera home installations to complex multi-site commercial security systems. Our 98% client satisfaction rate is our greatest achievement.
