FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Call us on 0743 088 275 and we'll answer any question.

How much does CCTV installation cost in Kenya?

CCTV installation costs in Kenya depend on the number and type of cameras. A basic 4-camera system for a home starts from KSh 18,000 – 35,000, while commercial 8–16 camera systems range from KSh 40,000 – 120,000+. Prices include cameras, DVR/NVR, cabling, connectors, and professional installation. We provide free site surveys and detailed written quotes at no charge.

What is the cost of electric fence installation?

Electric fence costs depend on the perimeter length and wire strands. Residential fencing starts from about KSh 1,200 – 2,000 per running metre installed, including energizer, earth stakes, warning signs, and all hardware. A typical ¼ acre home fence (roughly 80m) costs approximately KSh 95,000 – 180,000 depending on specification. We offer solar-powered energizer options for off-grid properties.

Do you provide maintenance services after installation?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance contracts for all our installations. Our standard service contract includes quarterly inspections, camera cleaning, system health checks, firmware updates, and priority emergency response. Maintenance contracts start from KSh 3,000 per month for small systems. Ad-hoc service calls are also available.

Can I view my CCTV cameras remotely on my phone?

Absolutely. All our CCTV installations support remote mobile viewing via free apps (Hik-Connect, DMSS, or similar). As long as your DVR/NVR is connected to the internet, you can view live footage, playback recordings, and receive motion alerts from anywhere in the world. We configure the entire remote access setup as part of the installation at no extra cost.

How long does a typical CCTV installation take?

Most residential CCTV installations (4–8 cameras) are completed in a single day, usually 4 to 8 hours. Larger commercial systems with 16+ cameras may take 2–3 days. We'll give you a precise timeline during your free site survey. Our team comes fully equipped, minimising disruption to your property.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Yes, we serve both residential and commercial clients with equal professionalism. Our commercial projects include supermarkets, factories, schools, hotels, apartments, hospitals, and offices. For large projects, we provide a dedicated project manager and work to agreed milestones. We are also available for government and NGO contracts, call us to discuss your specific requirements.

Is electric fencing legal in Kenya?

Yes, electric fencing is completely legal in Kenya when properly installed according to ERC (Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority) guidelines. All our electric fence installations comply with Kenyan safety standards including proper earthing, appropriate voltage levels, and mandatory warning signs. We handle all compliance documentation as part of our service.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes. Every installation comes with a 12-month workmanship warranty covering any faults in our installation work. Equipment is covered by the manufacturer's warranty (typically 1–3 years depending on the product). If anything goes wrong within the warranty period due to our work, we fix it at no charge. This commitment is put in writing before any project begins.
